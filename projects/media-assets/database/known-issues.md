
## 3D Game Character (not a specific NFT)

TokenGators has a 3D game character — a plain green or blue naked gator used in
Otherside/metaverse promotional content. It does NOT correspond to any specific NFT
and has no traits (no hat, outfit, etc.). The AI matcher may incorrectly link these
images to a Green or Blue NFT.

Known example tweets:
- tweet-2028674665099710699 (green gator, Otherside)
- tweet-2014765504838439072 (green/blue gator, Otherside promo)
